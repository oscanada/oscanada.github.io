Montreal, October 9–10, 2025

The 1st Canadian Conference on Open Science and Open Scholarship is a landmark event advancing open, transparent, and collaborative research across Canada. Hosted by Concordia University and partner institutions, the conference comes at a crucial moment, as Canada rapidly expands its commitment to open science through new mandates, platforms, and services. The event will bring together stakeholders from government, academia, and research to shape national guidelines and forge partnerships that will redefine how research is conducted and shared.

More information about the conference—including the call for contributions, keynote speakers, full program, and registration details—will be released soon. Be sure to check back for updates and opportunities to get involved!
