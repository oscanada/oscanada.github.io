The 1st Canadian Conference on Open Science and Open Scholarship will take place in Montreal on October 9–10, 2025. This first-of-its-kind national gathering will drive the adoption of open science practices across the country, making research more accessible, transparent, and collaborative. Hosted by Concordia University and partner institutions, the conference comes at a crucial moment, as Canada rapidly expands its commitment to open science through new mandates, platforms, and services. The event will bring together 150–200 stakeholders from government, academia, and research organizations to create guidelines and build partnerships that will transform how research is conducted and shared across Canada.

More information about the 1st Canadian Conference on Open Science and Open Scholarship—including the call for contributions, keynote speakers, full program, and registration details—will be released soon. Be sure to check back for updates and opportunities to get involved!
